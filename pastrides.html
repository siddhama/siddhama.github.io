Round Trip: IIT Mandi-Doohki-Pandoh-Bajaura-Kandhi-IIT Mandi


A day long ride with a stretch of 125 km with approx 2500 m elevation gain tests riders' endurance level. Pro level is required to complete the round trip. Toughest part being the climb of 1200m from Bajaura to Kandhi after a 78 km of ride and having gained elevation of 1300 m. Stay hydrated and don't forget to have lunch somewhere in between. Few places for sightseeing are Pandoh Dam, Hanogi Devi Temple & Bridge, River flowing alongside road near Aut & Bajaura, Bajaura valley enroute to Kandhi Pass, Kamand Valley from Kandhi Pass etc. Backpack: Multi-toolkit, Puncture kit, Front & Back Light(to cross Tunnel near Aut), Fruits especially banana, Dry fruits and approx 6 ORS packets.

Loop: Kamand-Doohki-Gharan-Mandi-Kathindi-Kamand


50 km loop from Kamand via Doohki-Gharan-Mandi. We climb upto Doohki followed by a steep downhill to Gharan where we follow Kullu-Mandi Highway till Mandi and then Kathindi Climb comes which becomes difficult if the rider is exhausted.

Parashar Lake


Parashar takes much effort from rider side. One can divide this ride in 4 segments with stop at Baghi, One Love Cafe, Bogchi Cafe. One Love cafe to Bogchi cafe has a steep climb through forest. Be careful to refill your water at each stop. No water source available after one love cafe.

Kamand-Kathindi-Drang-Mandi-Skore Loop

Loop: Kamand-Kathindi-Drang-Mandi-Skore-Kamand


After steep climb to Kathini take right on the Drang road to met with a downhill road. From Drang to MAndi we need to take MAndi-Jogindernagar highway so be careful while on the highway. Take exit to Skore from mandi it lies around 500m before Bhuli bridge. Road is paved till Skore but after that real offroad starts with no one around it. Refill yourself at skore no help available afterwards. Keep essential toolkit with you if planning to take this road.
